The CCP Connection: This is one of the project ideas that luanched me into computer programming. The current process for high school students signing up for college classes is composed of many pieces of paper and what seems like an endless stream of emails. The CCP Connection is a web application that intends to bring all stakeholders to one, single online portal to manage the entire course request process from start to finish.
